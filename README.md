# crochet.ya

> A complete online storefront and management system built for a handmade crochet business.

---

## What This Is

**crochet.ya** is a custom web application built for a small handmade crochet business. It gives customers a beautiful, easy-to-use shop to browse and order products — and gives the business owner a powerful private dashboard to manage everything without needing any technical knowledge.

Every piece on the store is handcrafted and one-of-a-kind, and the app was designed to reflect that warmth.

---

## What's Inside

### 🛍 For Customers

- Browse the full product catalog, organized by category
- View product details with photos, color/size variants, and pricing
- Add items to a cart or save them to a wishlist
- Place an order with a simple form — name, address, phone, and any notes
- Receive a clean order confirmation page after submitting

### 🔐 For the Shop Owner

A password-protected admin panel — only you can access it.

- **Dashboard** — at-a-glance stats: total products, orders placed, revenue earned, and recent activity
- **Products** — add, edit, or remove products; upload photos; set prices, variants, and categories
- **Orders** — view every incoming order with full customer details; update order status as it progresses
- **Categories** — control how your 6 product categories appear in the shop
- **Settings** — update your shop name, tagline, contact info, Instagram handle, and announcement banner

---

## How Orders Work

```
Customer browses the shop
       ↓
Adds items to cart and fills in the order form
       ↓
Order is saved and confirmed instantly
       ↓
You receive a full order summary on Telegram — immediately
```

No payment gateway required. Orders are placed as cash-on-delivery / manual fulfilment, keeping the process simple and personal.
